About Me

My name is Tomas Bejholt, and my path into tech hasn't been straight, but it has shaped me into the driven and solution‑oriented developer I am today. I started working early and have experience in construction, moving services, distribution, and running my own business in the trucking industry. It wasn't until I studied CAD/CAM and CNC programming that I discovered how naturally technology, logic, and digital creation came to me.

That insight motivated me to complete both primary and secondary school through Komvux, and I reached the point where I was only a few credits short of qualifying for a technical foundation year at KTH. Life took a different turn and I returned to the workforce, but my interest in technology never faded. After many years in physically demanding jobs, I decided to shift toward something sustainable, creative, and future‑focused.

Today, I am studying PIA25 – Python Programming in AI at Nackademin (August 2025 – May 2027). The program gives me a broad and modern foundation in system development and AI, with courses such as:

  • Python Programming
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Machine Learning – Theory & Practical Application

  • DevOps
  • Web Frameworks in Python
  • Business Skills
  • Thesis Project + LIA (internship)

I truly enjoy studying with my classmates and feel that I am finally in the right place. I am now looking for an LIA internship where I can continue growing, contribute with my drive, and learn from experienced developers. I look forward to joining a team and helping create something meaningful together.
